After Effects

Malaysia Theme

This promotional video celebrates Malaysia’s Independence Day by blending dynamic motion design with an energetic soundtrack to honor the nation's heritage. The project utilizes a range of digital synthesis techniques, including Track Matte and Alpha Masking for cinematic text reveals, Advanced Masking to integrate national landmarks with the flag, and Dynamic Scene Transitions that align architectural layers with moving elements. Additionally, the production features Sky Blending for symbolic visual storytelling, Kinetic Typography for professional title reveals, and Lumetri Color grading to ensure a vibrant, consistent aesthetic throughout the film.
