AR Storybook: Diner Escape

Project Overview

Diner Escape is an innovative AR mobile application that transforms a traditional children's storybook into a multi-sensory, interactive experience. By leveraging augmented reality, the project bridges the gap between physical reading and digital gaming, allowing readers to assist a family as they succumb to the irresistible aroma of a local diner. The story follows Mia, Jack, Papa, and Mama as they "escape" their household chores to follow a delicious scent through the city.

AR Interactive Marker

AR Business &Birthday Card

Project Overview

This project redefines traditional paper-based communication by overlaying digital layers onto physical cards. Using AR technology, I transformed a standard Business Card into a dynamic portfolio hub and a Birthday Card into a multi-sensory celebratory experience.

01. AR Business Card

Designed to make a lasting impression in professional networking, this card serves as a "living resume. Spatial UI buttons that allow recruiters to instantly jump to my LinkedIn, send a WhatsApp message, or draft an Email is integrated. A virtual screen floating above the card plays my latest design works, providing a frictionless viewing experience.

02. AR Birthday Card

The birthday card focused on emotional UX, turning a simple greeting into a memorable digital event. Scanning the card triggers festive 3D assets, spatial music, and animated elements that bring the celebration to life in the user's environment. This demonstrates how AR can be used for bespoke storytelling and enhancing personal milestones beyond the limitations of print.

AR Development

Built with Unity 3D and Vuforia Engine for high-precision image tracking and stable content overlay.

UI/UX & Interaction

Designed the spatial user interface, focusing on button affordance and intuitive touch interactions in a 3D space.

Cross-Platform Optimization

Ensured smooth performance on mobile devices, focusing on rapid tracking initialization and asset loading.
