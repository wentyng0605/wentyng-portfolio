Project Overview

The Echoes of Elysium is a 3D atmospheric adventure prototype that explores the profound connection between sound and life. AR Interactive Storybook: Diner Escape

Project Overview

Diner Escape is an innovative AR mobile application that transforms a traditional children's storybook into a multi-sensory, interactive experience. By leveraging augmented reality, the project bridges the gap between physical reading and digital gaming, allowing readers to assist a family as they succumb to the irresistible aroma of a local diner. The story follows Mia, Jack, Papa, and Mama as they "escape" their household chores to follow a delicious scent through the city.

Core AR Features & Interactions

Gamified Storytelling

Each character's journey is paired with a unique mini-game, such as a Math Challenge with Papa or rhythm-based tasks to progress through the town.

Interactive Narrative

Players don't just read; they actively "help" characters navigate streets and overcome obstacles to reach their goal.

Audio-Visual Immersion

The experience features synchronized voice-over narration and sound effects that trigger as AR content appears, enhancing the emotional connection to the story.
