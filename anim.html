The Legacy To A True Man

Project Overview

"The Legacy To A True Man" is a high-fantasy 3D cinematic short film that tells a story of ambition, lineage, and personal growth. Set in a world divided between two powerful forces—the noble Harrysons and the formidable Kingdom of Aldhave—the narrative follows a young prince named Arche Harryson.Trapped within a "gilded cage" of royal duty, Arche dreams of a life beyond castle walls, leading him on a dangerous journey where he must prove his worth to claim his true legacy.
