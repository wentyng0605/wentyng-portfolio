The Sakura Skyhouse

Wonder how it feels living above the cloud? This virtual environment -- Sakura Skyhouse is built high above the clouds. It is designed to evoke the feeling of "a hidden paradise beyond the world", allows users to immerse themselves in a tranquil, floating sanctuary.

With a soft purple color palette, handcrafted cloud formations, and self-modeled sakura trees and structures using Maya, the environment creates a dreamlike, meditative atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and exploration.

Moonlit Jing Guan Temple

A reimagined environment, envisioned as a peaceful sanctuary hidden away from the ordinary world. The scene takes place on the full moon of fifteenth night of the lunar month.

The project began with gathering visual references and conceptual inspiration from Lotus Pier and Chang’an to establish the direction of the environment. The terrain was then created and sculpted using brushes to shape the lake, central island, and surrounding landscape. Key assets were imported and placed with attention to composition, balance, and storytelling. Lighting became a major focus in refining the atmosphere. Moonlight, bloom, and reflections were adjusted to evoke a calm night setting. The floating Kongming lanterns were created using particle effects to add motion and a sense of celebration.

ENVIRONMENT DESIGN

Mayan Temple

The Mayan-themed battleground utilised a dark, atmospheric setting. The overall atmosphere and aesthetic is enhanced by flames and torches, casting eerie light across ancient stone structures. The map is designed for strategic FPS combat, includes elevated vantage points for long-range shooting, hidden cover spots for tactical movement, and a central arena for intense face-to-face battles.

Built entirely using Unity’s ProBuilder, this level balances verticality, cover, and engagement zones, creating a dynamic and immersive combat environment.
