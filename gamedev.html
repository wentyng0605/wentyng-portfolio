Action-Adventure: The Fallen Olympus

Project Overview

The Fallen Olympus is a mythological action-adventure built in Unity, where players step into the role of Ares, the fallen God of War. Unbeknownst to Ares, he is a pawn in a grander scheme: Cronus, the Titan King, is secretly manipulating his journey to seize the Emblem’s power for his own dark ambitions.

First-Person Shooting Game: The Solaria

Project Overview

On a mysterious, cursed island, once-human monsters roam, their bodies twisted by an ancient curse. Deep within this desolate land lies Solaria, a legendary flower rumored to possess the power to cure any illness.

To save his beloved, the protagonist embarks on a perilous journey to retrieve the flower. As players navigate this abandoned realm, they must unravel the island’s dark secrets while surviving the horrors that dwell within.

My Role: Team Leader

  • - I managed the team’s development pipeline, ensuring high-quality execution from concept to final build.
  • - Designed and programmed interactive puzzles and dialogue interactions.
  • - Developed in-game cinematics using Cinemachine and Timeline for immersive storytelling.

The game explores the theme of the subconscious, featuring ever-changing dreamscapes filled with both beauty and hidden dangers.
