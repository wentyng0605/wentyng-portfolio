Project Overview

The Fallen Olympus is a mythological action-adventure built in Unity, where players step into the role of Ares, the fallen God of War. Unbeknownst to Ares, he is a pawn in a grander scheme: Cronus, the Titan King, is secretly manipulating his journey to seize the Emblem’s power for his own dark ambitions.

The Sakura Skyhouse

Unity 3D Modeling VR Space Design

Wonder how it feels living above the cloud? This virtual (VR) environment -- Sakura Skyhouse is built high above the clouds. It is designed to evoke the feeling of "a hidden paradise beyond the world", allows users to immerse themselves in a tranquil, floating sanctuary using a VR device.

With a soft purple color palette, handcrafted cloud formations, and self-modeled sakura trees and structures using Maya, the environment creates a dreamlike, meditative atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and exploration.

Moonlit Jing Guan Temple

Unreal Engine Cinematics Level Design

A peaceful sanctuary inspired by the aesthetics of Lotus Pier and ancient Chang’an. Set under the fifteenth lunar moon, this environment features handcrafted terrains and a sculpted central lake. By orchestrating volumetric moonlight with dynamic particle-effect lanterns, the scene evokes a calm, celebratory atmosphere perfect for immersive exploration.

Mayan Temple

Unity ProBuilder Level Design

A dark, atmospheric battleground built entirely with Unity’s ProBuilder to balance verticality and tactical engagement. The aesthetic is driven by dynamic torchlight casting eerie shadows across ancient stone structures，enhancing the immersive FPS experience. Designed for strategic combat，the map features elevated vantage points，hidden tactical cover，and a high-intensity central arena.
